Animation

The stuff in motion. It's a step by step process that can be very tedious, but absolutely satisfying and rewarding in the end. Anything requiring animation can become a big project fairly quickly. There would be a lot to discuss, plan, and be on the same page about when tackling one of these projects.

I do VFX as well, i.e. working with green screens and motion tracking. Correctly set up scenes and storyboard are required, as is communication between myself and the director. You can find three fully animated visuals here, along with two music videos that heavily utilize 3D graphics and CGI.

Motion visuals require multiple processes to complete. Projects can range from a workload of 20-40 hours, to 4-8 weeks worth. The length of the visual itself plays a big part in pricing. It's also dependent on things like the amount of scenes needed, number of modeled character(s), lip-syncing, VFX/motion tracking, and types of physical simulations (explosions, liquids, smoke and fire). Pricing can increase quickly, so there is a lot to discuss for these projects. Make sure to get in contact with me with any info you can provide, so I can figure out exactly what you're looking for and nail down an accurate quote.
