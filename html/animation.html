Animation

The stuff in motion. It's a step by step process that can be very tedious, but absolutely satisfying and rewarding. Anything requiring animation can become a big project fairly quickly. So there would be a lot to discuss, plan, and be on the same page about when tackling one of these projects.

I do VFX as well, working with green screens and motion tracking. Correctly set up scenes and storyboard are required, so I would need to be in touch with the director of the visual. You can find three fully animated visuals here, along with two music videos that are heavily implemented with 3D graphics and CGI.

Motion visuals require multiple processes to complete. Projects can range from a workload of 20-40 hours, to 4-8 weeks worth. The length of the visual itself plays a big part in pricing. It's also dependant on things like the amount of scenes needed, number of modeled character(s), lip-syncing, and types of physical simulations (explosions, liquids, smoke and fire). Pricing can increase quickly, so there is a lot to discuss for these projects. If what your looking for doesn't require these types of attributes/effects that I mentioned, it may be priced differently due to a different size in workload. I havent even begun to talk about VFX with green screens and motion tracking. Make sure to get in contact with me with any info you can provide, so I can figure out exactly what your looking for and narrow down a more specific quote.
