3D
Artwork

Still art: covers, banners, portraits, and other types of 3D imagery. Here I chose to showcase cover art - it's the category that has allowed me to really show what I can do. Most of these pieces also came with extra photo packs and visuals for promotion. The characters in each piece can be accompanied with their own head shots and used in multiple projects.

My work can fall into a niche usually with music artists. In this space I'm allowed tons of creative freedom. Putting together artwork that goes with a story or vibe of the music is a fun challenge. This isn't the only avenue that can be taken. 3D works great for Twitch streamers, as website graphics, brand mockups for promotion. It's an innovative way to promote your merch, brand, or self.

I like to dabble in different styles from minimalism to jam-packed scenes. The art chosen for this page is some of my most recent favorites. If you head to my Instagram you'll get to see all the different projects that I've worked on, along with my logo/brand work and clips from animations. If you like what you see or have any questions, let me know. If you're a fellow artist or designer I'm always looking to grow the network!

I work with most budgets depending on the concept and how in depth it is. Artwork consisting of modeled character(s) tend to be higher in price, same with sceneries that require very busy settings (like city streets). When dealing with a more simplified concept, we can stretch budgets further. Logo work starts in 2D vector art, and can be brought into the 3D space. Cover art can be accompanied with a loop visual for Spotify, or a full visualizer for YouTube. Same goes for brand reveal animations for your new logo.
