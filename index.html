About Me

My name is Max, you can call me Munk. I've been a digital artist for 10+ years starting from not-so-good short films & photo manipulation. This turned into directing and editing music videos mixed with photography. I went to school for graphic design on and off, then dropped out with an amount of credits where I should have my BA. It wasn't exactly what I was looking to do but I love the experience I gained. Learning in various mediums has really helped shape my techniques, how I work, and what I like to use. That's also where I picked up Illustrator, now using it for logo & design work. I'm also efficient in other Adobe products such as Photoshop and After effects. I use them as daily tools implemented into my 3D art. I always loved animation and the classic movies, of course from studios like Pixar. Eventually I landed on the software Blender, and instantly dived into the 3D space. I gravitated towards working with hip-hop artists and indie clothing brands, and eventually was able to turn my freelance work into my full time job. You can see my work across the main social medias, and dont hesistate to get in contact with me if your interested!

Pricing Criteria

Pricing is project specific, but will usually fall within the same general range depending on the type of artwork/visual you are looking for. Pricing is based off an estimated amount of hours that is calculated by comparing it to the average of my past pieces. Imagery like cover art and portraits would be in a different pricing bracket than animation and VFX.
